4. What are fertilizers used for in aquaculture?

5. What are the types of fertilizers used in aquaculture?

Fertilizers in aquaculture can be classified into:

Organic fertilizers, such as manure, fish meal, and seaweed, which are rich in nutrients and environmentally friendly

Micronutrient fertilizers that help in addressing deficiencies of specific nutrients like zinc, iron, or manganese.

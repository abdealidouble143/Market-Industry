USB charging lighters, also known as electric or plasma lighters, are a modern alternative to traditional gas or flint lighters. These devices function by using an internal rechargeable battery that powers a heating element or a plasma arc, making them windproof, eco-friendly, and reusable. Unlike conventional lighters, USB charging lighters can be powered through standard USB ports and are designed for sustainability and convenience, catering to a range of users including smokers, outdoor enthusiasts, and households.
